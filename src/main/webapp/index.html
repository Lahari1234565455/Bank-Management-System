Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use both PayPal and Stripe?

Yes, our platform supports multiple payment gateways including PayPal and Stripe for seamless transactions.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day refund policy on all subscription plans. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

How do I upgrade my plan?

Upgrading is easy! Log into your account, choose the desired plan, and complete the payment. All your previous settings and data remain intact.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we provide a 14-day free trial for new users so you can explore our services before committing to a plan.

Where are you located?

Our headquarters are located in New York, but our services are available globally online.

What are your working hours?

Our customer support is available Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM EST.

Can I change my payment method?

Yes, you can update or change your payment method anytime through your account settings.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. All fees are clearly listed in each plan and there are no hidden charges. What you see is what you pay.
